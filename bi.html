Addressing Phone Dependency

Project Overview
This is a design challenge I completed for a HCD organization at UC Berkeley. I was presented with the initial design question: "While smartphones keep us connected virtually, research suggests that smartphone usage actually harms our mannerisms and the quality of our in-person social interactions. With this in mind, this semester’s design challenge is: How might we encourage better social habits?"
