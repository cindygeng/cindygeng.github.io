Water Bottle Brush Design

Project Overview
Hey there! The link below leads to a case study I worked on for a Human-Centered course. I created a more efficient and effective water bottle brush.
Duration
3+ months

Electric water bottle brush

People take their reusable water bottles everywhere; it's convenient and sustainable. However, many users aren't satisfied with their experiences with available cleaning products and thus, avoid cleaning their bottles as much as possible. This is unsanitary and could result in health concerns. Although bottles usually only hold water, they still accumulate germs and bacteria.
