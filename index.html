Hi, nice to meet you! My name is Cindy Geng, I'm a third-year undergraduate student at UC Berkeley studying Cognitive Science. My first two years of undergrad involved a lot of trial and error -- I wasn't sure what my career goals were. I love psychology and learning about how our minds work, so I tried my hand at a psych/pre-med major. Then, I started gaining interest in the tech industry (I always attribute it to the Bay Area influence). Long story short, I found a way to combine both of these passions and fell in love with the idea of intersecting psychology and tech/design. Thanks for reading! Check out my work if you're interested and let me know what you think. :)
